The 48-Hour Puppy Survival Protocol

Your hour-by-hour checklist to catch problems before they become emergencies.

Why the first 48 hours matter

Neonatal mortality is highest in the first two days. This protocol is designed to provide a structured assessment framework. Follow these checkpoints rigorously to ensure your litter thrives.
